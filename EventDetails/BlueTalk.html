BLUETALK

Bluetalk was an awareness program organized to create awareness about the deteriorating state of our environment. A presentation was delivered by the members of Hourglass, an ‘Ocean Pollution – The Greatest Tragedy of our Generation’. It was not just an event, but an revolutionary event with an aim to create awareness. The event also witnessed the formation of a human in the shape of the word ‘OCEAN’ and was captured with the help of a drone.
